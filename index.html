The Pianist Showcase

Hi! My name is Jared Barker, and I love playing the piano, and enjoying the music of other pianists. Here at the Pianist Showcase, I like to share some of my recent favorites. I also feature certain pianists, and share some of my own work as well.

Featured Songs

This is 'Blackheart' composed by Two Steps From Hell, arranged and played by Patrik Pietschmann. I love Two Steps From Hell's epic music, and Patrik does an amazing job arranging it for piano.

This is an arrangement of the soundtract to the movie 'How to Train Your Dragon'. Again, Patrik Pietschmann does a beautiful arrangement of an already beautiful score.

This is 'Don't Stop Believin'' as arranged by Jarrod Radnich. I love Jarrod's epic and awesome covers, and it's even better when it's already a great song.
