Jared Barker

I am by no means a professional pianist like Jon Schmidt and Marshall McDonald, but I still want to share some of my pieces and arrangements. I first learned to play the piano from my siblings, and I learned by watching them, not reading sheet music. It wasn't until late in my high school years that I started to fully understand sheet music, and I started composing my own music around that time.

This song does not have a name, as I can't find a title that I feel represents it well. Once when I was trying to actually write sheet music for this song, I used the title 'Lake,' but I have don't particularly like this title. I wrote this song while serving in the Arkansas Bentonville Mission

This song also does not have a title, and I also composed this on my mission. The tune kind of popped into my head, and I quickly worked it out on the piano. I really like the happy and nostalgic feeling from this song.

This song is an original arrangement of the hymns "Come Thou Fount of Every Blessing" and "Be Thou My Vision." I love the melodies of these songs, and when written in the same key, they flow in and out of each other well. I originally arranged them separately, and then combined them spontaneously after noticing how the melodies fit well with each other.
