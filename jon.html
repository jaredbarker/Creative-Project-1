Jon Schmidt

Jon Schmidt is one of my favorite pianists. I first learned about him from The Piano Guys, and have learned 'All of Me' and 'Waterfall'. He has a unique style that he calls "New-age classical." Here are some links to buy sheet music, and see more of his work.

This is 'All of Me,' composed and played by Jon Schmidt. This is the first piece of his that I learned, after my brother asked me to. I love the energetic and beautiful melody, and the parts where you play with your arm are especially fun.

This is 'Waterfall,' composed and played by Jon Schmidt. I learned this piece after All of Me, and while I think I still like All of me more, this is basically tied. It is so beautiful and flowing, and is very enjoyable to play.

This is me playing Jon Schmidt's arrangement of the hymn 'Onward Christian Soldiers.' I only play the first verse; the song continues on very majestically. You can listen to the whole piece here.
